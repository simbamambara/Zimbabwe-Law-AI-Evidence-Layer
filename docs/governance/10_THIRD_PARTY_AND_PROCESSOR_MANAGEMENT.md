# Third-Party and Processor Management

Before using cloud hosting, AI APIs, contractors, analytics, or backup providers:
- define controller and processor roles;
- perform due diligence;
- sign data-processing and confidentiality terms;
- restrict purpose and sub-processing;
- address location and transfers;
- require security, incident, deletion, and audit commitments;
- maintain an exit plan and evidence of deletion.

Use `docs/registers/third_party_processor_register.csv`.
