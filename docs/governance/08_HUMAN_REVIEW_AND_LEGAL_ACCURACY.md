# Human Review and Legal Accuracy

No structured draft is approved automatically.

Review stages:
1. Source identity and judgment-number verification.
2. Official publication-classification verification.
3. Court, parties, judges, and dates verification.
4. Headnote, catchwords, ratio, and held-point legal review.
5. Citation and legislation review.
6. Privacy and protected-identity review.
7. Editorial approval signature.
8. Release validation and manifest generation.

Use `docs/registers/publication_approval_register.csv`. Reviewer comments and approval status must be retained.
