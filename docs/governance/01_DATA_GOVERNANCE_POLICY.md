# Data Governance Policy

## Scope
Zimbabwe Law AI datasets, source judgments, derived metadata, logs, models, indexes, backups, and evidence packages.

## Roles
- Data owner: approves purpose, access, and release.
- Data Protection Officer: advises, monitors, trains, assesses risk, and coordinates regulatory and data-subject matters.
- Legal editorial lead: verifies source accuracy and publication status.
- Technical custodian: implements access, integrity, backup, and change controls.
- Users: process data only for authorised purposes.

## Principles
Purpose limitation, data minimisation, accuracy, storage limitation, integrity, confidentiality, accountability, human review, and traceability.

## Mandatory controls
Classify data, approve access, log material changes, retain source hashes, review releases, respond to corrections, test backups, manage incidents, and reassess high-risk processing.

## Approval
This policy is a draft until signed by management, the data owner, the DPO, and legal counsel.
