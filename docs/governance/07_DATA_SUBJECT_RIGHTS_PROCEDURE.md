# Data Subject Rights Procedure

Requests are logged, identity is verified proportionately, scope is clarified, systems are searched, exemptions are assessed, and a documented response is issued within the applicable period.

Supported request types include access, correction, objection, restriction or suppression, and deletion where applicable. Court-record integrity, public-interest obligations, legal holds, and publication mandates require legal review.

Use `docs/registers/data_subject_request_register.csv`. Never alter an official source judgment; corrections to derived records must preserve provenance and release history.
