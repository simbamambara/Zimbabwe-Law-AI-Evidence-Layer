# Security Incident and Breach Response

## Immediate steps
1. Detect and record the incident.
2. Contain access without destroying evidence.
3. Preserve logs, hashes, and affected file versions.
4. Notify the incident lead and DPO.
5. Assess data categories, subjects, scale, harm, and jurisdictions.
6. Decide regulatory and data-subject notification with legal advice and current official requirements.
7. Eradicate the cause, restore safely, and monitor.
8. Document lessons and corrective actions.

Do not include personal information in public tickets. Use `docs/registers/incident_register.csv`. Notification deadlines and content must be verified against current law and POTRAZ guidance at the time of the incident.
