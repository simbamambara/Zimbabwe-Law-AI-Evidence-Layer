# Data Retention and Deletion Policy

- Raw source evidence: retain according to the legal and editorial mandate and approved archival schedule; restrict access.
- Normalized working dataset: retain while the project is active and for the approved audit period.
- Public derivatives: versioned; withdraw or supersede when correction or suppression is required.
- Logs: retain only as long as needed for security, accountability, and incident investigation.
- Backups: expire on a documented rotation; deletion must propagate through active systems and scheduled backup expiry.
- Legal holds override routine deletion and must be documented.

Use `docs/registers/retention_schedule.csv`. Destruction must be approved and evidenced.
