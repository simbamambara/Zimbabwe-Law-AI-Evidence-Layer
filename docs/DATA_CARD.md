# Evidence Dataset Card

## Purpose

Legal editorial research, retrieval evaluation, dataset-quality analysis, compliance evidence, and controlled development of Zimbabwe-focused legal information systems.

## Composition

522 canonical records: 465 structured draft records, 51 exclusions, and 6 manual-review records.

Court coverage: Constitutional Court (10), Supreme Court (119), and High Court (393). High Court station counts are Harare 272, Bulawayo 95, Masvingo 19, Chinhoyi 6, and Mutare 1.

## Unit of observation

One canonical judgment or workflow record with all source occurrences retained for provenance.

## Sensitive content

Source records may include names, addresses, family matters, health information, criminal allegations, minors, or protected identities. Automated scans are preliminary and do not replace human review.

## Known limitations

- Structured drafts are not official approved publications.
- Source material may contain transcription, spelling, and formatting artefacts.
- Classification anomalies require source-heading verification.
- Coverage reflects the supplied processed material, not a guarantee of every judgment delivered in 2022.
- Derived term arrays may split complex legal citations imperfectly.
- Legal relevance and precedential status require professional review.

## Prohibited interpretation

A record's presence does not imply official approval, legal correctness, publication permission, or suitability for automated decision-making.
