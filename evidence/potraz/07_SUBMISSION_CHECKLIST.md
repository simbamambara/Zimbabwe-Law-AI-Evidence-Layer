# Submission Checklist

I will use this checklist before I treat the package as ready for filing.

- [ ] I have completed the organisation's legal name, registration number, and address.
- [ ] I have confirmed the data-controller and processor classification.
- [ ] I have retrieved the current POTRAZ forms and fees from the official source.
- [ ] I have completed the relevant data-controller registration or licence application.
- [ ] The DPO has been formally appointed and current contact information is included.
- [ ] The ROPA has been reviewed and approved.
- [ ] The DPIA has been approved and the residual risk has been accepted.
- [ ] I have documented the lawful basis for processing.
- [ ] The full-text publication-rights analysis has been approved.
- [ ] The privacy notice has been approved.
- [ ] Processor agreements and transfer assessments have been completed.
- [ ] I have attached access-control and MFA evidence.
- [ ] I have attached security and backup-testing evidence.
- [ ] An incident-response exercise has been completed and recorded.
- [ ] Staff training records are attached.
- [ ] Manual-review and classification-anomaly queues are resolved or clearly disclosed.
- [ ] The latest validation report passes.
- [ ] The hash manifest has been verified.
- [ ] Management, DPO, legal, editorial, and security sign-off is complete.
- [ ] I have archived the final submission copy with its date and receipt or reference number.
